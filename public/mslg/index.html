The Ship

Sugar Hill ITC Tethys. Titan class C/10 long-hauler. Pods arrayed around a nuclear thruster core. Stasis pods bear colonists. Several pods are dedicated to the Exoplanet Diasporic Environment Node (EDEN), while others contain food, medicine, small vehicles, and an armory.

The Journey

Planned destination was one of several pre-identified exoplanets marked by previous probes as uninhabited.

The Crew

To save on life support costs, the flight crew consists of select members of the Extended Contractors Union, who agreed to indefinite reanimation as a means of supporting family left behind after their untimely demise. They cannot speak, but are tirelessly devoted to their mission.
