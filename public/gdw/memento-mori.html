Grumbl Gwoodwom

A mossling

A characteristic of mosslings is that they sort of appear unnoticed, such that parties suddenly or gradually become aware of them. Grumbl's passing was noticed, however, as he downed a foul crookhorn in Droomen Knoll before its companions overwhelmed him. He perished ere he could receive the ministrations of Goodsman Emory.
