Status: Active

Logistics

When: Saturdays from 7 PM to 10 PM Eastern (NYC). Seasonal from 7 September 2024 to 31 May 2025.

Quorum: DM plus three players. Absent players may come back to find they have been in Faerie for the previous session. Otherwise we proceed as if they had been there the whole time unless other circumstances pull them away.

Mode: Play is virtual, with the link to the Foundry VTT having been provided.

Coordination: Discord.
